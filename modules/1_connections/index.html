Connections

If you want to get healthier, you first need to understand what aspects of your behavior are connected to your health. These behaviors include diet, exercise, stress, sleep, relationships, sunlight, and so on.

The next step is to understand the strength of the connections. For example, diet is often an important component of health so changing your diet may have a larger effect on health than increasing the amount of sunlight you get.

Additionally, it's important to know the direction of the connection. Improving health may mean eating more or fewer calories depending on your current weight. Connections are inversely related when increases in one, leads to a decrease in another.
